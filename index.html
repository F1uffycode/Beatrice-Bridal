Welcome to the world of elegance and beauty

We are delighted to welcome you to our space, where dreams of the perfect wedding dress come true.
For over 25 years, our brand has been creating bridal gowns and shaping trends on the global wedding market.

Our journey began with the creation of bridal accessories, but as time and fashion evolved, so did we.

For more than 7 years, we have been dressing brides in happiness, creating gowns that highlight beauty while ensuring comfort and practicality on the most important day of their lives.

Our collaboration with bridal salons is built on trust, inspiration, and the shared goal of creating joyful and memorable experiences.

Traveling on business tours to bridal salons across the world

We identified a common trend — showroom windows are filled with beautiful, yet similar dresses.

We help our partners stand out and capture attention by creating exclusive, designer, and thematic bridal gowns tailored specifically for their salons. With a distinctive showcase comes visibility, recognition, and guaranteed success.

Today, Beatrice Bridal gowns are represented in 15 countries worldwide.

We continue to grow, evolve, and refine our collections, paying close attention to quality, craftsmanship, and every detail that matters.

By participating in leading international exhibitions, we share our vision and story with the global bridal community.

Venice | February 15-17
